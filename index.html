Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 29, 2026

Welcome to CHIEF. We are committed to protecting the privacy and data security of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how our mobile application (operating via a secure webview connection) handles your information when you access our platform, create an account, or engage with our digital registry services.

1. The Information We Collect

We believe in absolute data minimization. We only collect the specific details required to manage your account and watch authentication:

  • Account Profile Information: When you launch the application and set up an account, we collect your Name to establish and secure your profile.
  • Digital Watch Registry Data: For patrons who purchase a CHIEF watch, we securely store details including your Name, the watch's Unique Registry Code, and the specific Watch Edition to maintain permanent, digital ownership records.
  • Reward System Balance: We track the "Coins" earned and utilized through your in-app rewards activity.

2. Zero Device Permissions Policy

To ensure your ultimate privacy, our mobile application **does not** request, access, or track any hardware permissions from your device. This includes but is not limited to: Location tracking, Camera, Microphone, Storage/Media, Contacts, or Device IDs.

3. How We Utilize Your Data

Your data is strictly utilized for core app features to optimize your brand experience:

  • Digital Authenticity & Registry: Keeping your watch registry securely saved so you can check and prove which watch belongs to whose name digitally.
  • In-App Loyalty Program: Maintaining your permanent Coin balance so you can seamlessly earn rewards and redeem them for items in our store.
  • Profile Persistence: Ensuring your account dashboard and watch purchase history remain safely saved for lifetime verification.

4. Order Integration via WhatsApp

Our application utilizes a direct WhatsApp redirect for frictionless order processing. When you click the "Confirm Order" section within the app, you will be seamlessly directed to WhatsApp to complete your message manually. We do not automatically scrape or extract any external communication data or personal details from your WhatsApp application.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We respect your privacy. We do not sell, trade, rent, or lease your personal identification or watch registry details to third-party marketing companies, advertisers, or any outsiders.

6. User Rights & Fast Support

Every user has the right to access their digital assets and profile safely. We provide direct and expedited concierge assistance. If you ever need help, profile corrections, or have any questions about your stored data, you can connect with us directly.

7. Third-Party Advertising (AdMob)

This application uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use cookies and device identifiers to provide personalized ads. You can learn more about how Google uses data in their Privacy Policy.

The Journey Blog | Vol. I

Day One to One Day: The Mindset Behind CHIEF

When I entered the 12th grade, after facing constant setbacks and rejections from every direction, I realized the ultimate truth of this world—people do not respect you for who you are; they respect your financial status and your wealth. As a brand owner, it might sound strange or blunt to admit this, but it is the absolute truth. At that definitive moment, I decided that I didn't just want to study blindly to survive; I wanted to build an ecosystem where I create real value and command my own financial destiny.

The Dark Phase of Trials

I was originally a NEET Aspirant, dedicating my hours to studies. However, observing the outside world through digital media, it became clear to me that earning money through a standard job is completely different from building long-term generational wealth through a legacy brand. I asked myself a simple question: If other billionaires are human beings born with the same potential, why can't I do it too?

But saying it was effortless; executing it was an entirely different battle. I dove headfirst into content creation, risked stakes in the stock market, and spent an entire year experimenting everywhere—only to face absolute failure. During those dark hours, I came to understand that in today’s hyper-competitive and saturated market, surviving without a highly unique, disruptive vision is impossible.

The Turning Point & The Podcast

Everything shifted when I watched a profound podcast by Raj Shamani, explicitly discussing "How to create a thousand-crore brand." I observed every minute with intense focus and noted down a vital truth: If you want to enter a crowded market and win, you must execute something so radically different that no one has ever dared to attempt it before.

Leveraging my basic coding and development skills, I identified a massive gap. In today's market, even the highest-tier luxury watch brands suffer from advanced first-copies and replicas. Consumers pay premium prices but still risk holding a fake counterfeit. To eradicate this vulnerability forever, CHIEF was born.

The Innovation: Unreplicable Digital Twin

I engineered a system where every single watch leaves our facility with a completely Unique Verification Code permanently etched into its legacy. Anyone can try to replicate a physical design silhouette, but no one in the world can copy our secure digital registry database locked inside the mobile app!

This digital authentication guarantees that every timepiece is truly **"Only One Piece in the World."** Owners can instantly log into our application to verify precisely under whose name the watch is officially registered.

Making Luxury 100% Worth It

When I initially finalized the baseline price at ₹2,000, I stepped into the consumer's shoes and realized that for a student or an average individual, this might seem like a heavy premium. Therefore, I packed the offering with undeniable, top-tier assets to make it unconditionally worth every rupee:

★ Unique One-Piece Worldwide Guarantee
★ Bespoke Premium Luxury Packaging
★ Digital Ownership Certificate Card
★ 3 Times Completely Free Master Repairing Services
★ ₹200 Monthly Voucher (Fully customizable by yourself to redeem inside our store)
★ Custom Edition Design Adjustments built entirely to your taste
★ High-Priority Instant Support routing straight to WhatsApp

A Student’s Honest Manifestation

I want to leave you with one final, transparent truth. I am writing every single word of this manifesto while still sitting in a **12th-grade classroom**. As of today, my brand hasn't taken over physical stores globally yet—but this is my vision, my unyielding focus, and my ultimate dream. I am fully committed to turning this **"Day One"** into **"One Day"**. Thank you for reading my origin story. As a parting thought, remember—NEVER GIVE UP!
