Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 19, 2026

Welcome to CHIEF. This Privacy Policy explains how CHIEF ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the CHIEF mobile application and its related services.

CHIEF is designed to provide watch verification, registry services, rewards, voucher management, customised watch order requests, and related Chief Store services.

1. Information We Collect

CHIEF follows a data-minimisation approach and collects information necessary to provide its services.

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we may store your username and password or authentication credentials required to manage your account.
  • Authentication Information: Account authentication may be handled through Firebase-based services. A system-generated authentication identifier may be associated with your account for account management and security.
  • Watch Verification Information: When you verify an eligible CHIEF watch, the App may process the unique watch verification code and related registry information.
  • Watch Registry Information: With the user's permission, registry information may include the user-provided name, watch model, unique watch status, registry date, and other information necessary to maintain the CHIEF watch registry.
  • Rewards and C Coins: We may store information about daily rewards, referral rewards, free reward activities, and C Coins earned through eligible activities.
  • Voucher Information: We may store voucher type, purchase amount, activation status, monthly credit information, validity information, redemption status, and other information required to manage your voucher.
  • Payment Verification Information: If a voucher purchase requires transaction verification, the user may submit a UTR or transaction reference. This information may be stored for manual payment verification and voucher activation.
  • Order Information: Information entered by the user while customising or preparing a CHIEF watch order may be used to prepare and communicate the order request.

2. Device Permissions

CHIEF does not require access to your device's location, camera, microphone, contacts, or personal media files for basic account creation and watch verification services.

Certain third-party services used by the App, such as advertising services, may process technical information according to their own policies and applicable configurations.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Creating and managing user accounts;
  • Authenticating users and maintaining account security;
  • Providing CHIEF watch verification services;
  • Maintaining the CHIEF watch registry;
  • Managing daily rewards, referrals, and C Coins;
  • Verifying voucher transactions and activating vouchers;
  • Maintaining voucher credit and redemption records;
  • Preparing customised watch order requests;
  • Providing customer support;
  • Preventing fraud, abuse, and unauthorised activity;
  • Maintaining and improving the App and its services.

4. Watch Registry and Verification

CHIEF watches may contain a unique verification code that can be used to verify an eligible watch through the App.

When a user chooses to register or verify a watch, the App may associate registry information with the watch record, including the user's provided name, watch model, unique status, and registry date.

Users must not attempt to guess, generate, manipulate, duplicate, or misuse watch verification codes or registry records.

5. C Coins and Rewards

CHIEF may provide rewards through daily rewards, referral features, free reward activities, or other eligible activities.

C Coins are reward points and are not cash. C Coins cannot be purchased through the App, withdrawn as cash, or converted into cash.

Reward balances and C Coins may be adjusted, suspended, or removed where there is suspected fraud, abuse, technical error, duplicate activity, or violation of applicable terms.

6. Vouchers and Store Credit

CHIEF may offer vouchers such as Silver, Gold, and Diamond vouchers. Voucher information, including price, monthly credit, total limit, validity, discount, and other conditions, is displayed with the applicable voucher.

Voucher credit is intended only for eligible purchases of physical products from the CHIEF Store.

Voucher credit cannot be withdrawn as cash, converted into cash, transferred to a bank account or wallet, or used as general in-app money.

7. Payment Verification

Where a voucher purchase requires payment verification, the App may request a UTR or transaction reference from the user.

The submitted transaction reference may be stored and manually reviewed by CHIEF for payment verification and voucher activation.

CHIEF does not request or store your banking password, UPI PIN, ATM PIN, or card PIN.

8. Order Integration via WhatsApp

CHIEF may provide an order customisation feature that prepares an order summary based on information entered by the user.

When the user chooses to contact CHIEF through WhatsApp, the App may open WhatsApp and prepare a message containing the order information. The user can review the message before sending it.

CHIEF does not access, read, or extract unrelated private conversations from the user's WhatsApp account.

9. Third-Party Services

CHIEF may use third-party services and infrastructure to operate the App and its services, including cloud storage, authentication, hosting, advertising, and communication services.

CHIEF does not sell your personal information to third-party marketing companies.

10. Advertising Services

CHIEF may use Google AdMob or other advertising services to display advertisements and provide eligible reward advertisements.

Advertising providers may process certain technical, device, or advertising-related information according to their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

11. Data Storage and Security

CHIEF uses third-party cloud and hosting infrastructure, including Firebase-based services, to store and manage application information.

We take reasonable measures to protect information against unauthorised access, misuse, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

12. Account Deletion and Data Requests

Users may request deletion of their CHIEF account and associated personal account information through the account deletion method provided by CHIEF.

To request account deletion or ask questions about your stored data, please contact us using the contact details below.

Certain information may be retained where necessary for legal obligations, fraud prevention, security, dispute resolution, product authenticity records, or other lawful purposes.

13. Children's Privacy

CHIEF does not knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable law.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

CHIEF may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updated version may be made available through the App or the official CHIEF Privacy Policy page.
